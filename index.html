Who am I?

I can best describe myself as a person who spent 20 years in corporate life heading businesses, and 23 years transforming people to find meaning in what they do. We have till now trained and skilled 95+ brands, 65000+ people in areas of customer facing and people management. I have facilitated training workshops in India, China, SE Asia, Middle East, and Australia and have engaged in business dealings with customers in 30 plus countries and hence have rich experience, and an in-depth knowledge of people and cultures accross the world.

I am an Engineer and have been certified with 'Senior Management' courses in IIM Ahmedabad and TMTC Pune. Before I turned entrepreneur, I worked with large multinational organisations like Kennametal Widia and Saint Gobain .

Presently I am a Founder Director and CEO in  Sabre Learning and Skilling Systems  which is engaged in corporate training in Retail, Hospitality and Engineering sectors supported with our home grown Learning Management System. 

I am the co-author of the book ‘Above and Beyond – How to build Impactful business where everyone wins’. 

I have also authored the Amazon top 50 business bestseller - "Dont Sell, Make them Buy" published by The Book Bakers and Om Books International.

Being an avid animal conservationist,  photo enthusiast and wildlife lover, I spend all my spare time vacationing in places which have less people and more animals. P resently, I live in Mysuru and in my farm house in Kabini, Karnataka.
